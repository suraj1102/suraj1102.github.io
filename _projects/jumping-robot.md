> Course: ME3001: Engineering Mechanics

The objective of the project was to design a jumping / hopping robot. The detailed **[report can be found here](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1jpATiYn4C-Pz4TEwadlm_nwABOdWph0JAyLvsSU40m4/edit?usp=sharing)**

### Design Constraints:
- Jump Height: 3 cm
- Number of continuous jumps: 3 minimum
- Robot Footprint: less than 100 cm²
- Cost: less than ₹1500 ($16)
